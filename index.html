The Journey

Milestones

Eleven splits. Four launches. Hundreds of decisions. Tap any checkpoint to open the full story.

Split Month Checkpoint Status
01 Mar 2025 SOW Finalized — Race Begins ✓ Complete

SOW finalised for the D7/WP → D11 Managed Migration across four Race Forward sites. Alignment on UAT timelines, invoicing milestones, and support scope locked with Timothy Armstrong. The starting gun had fired.

02 Apr – Jun 2025 Active Delivery + AI Migration Pilot ✓ Complete

Active delivery across 4 sites simultaneously. Team began experimenting with AI-assisted code migration (Codex, Cline + OpenRouter) — an Axelerant first. Estimated 20–25% reduction in backend effort, with gains tracked and shared openly.

03 Apr – Jul 2025 Facing Race Launch ✓ Complete

First major launch: Facing Race Podcast Platform goes live on Drupal 11. Complex legacy WordPress to Drupal migration — performed flawlessly with zero downtime. Ajaypal shared the win in #celebrate to the whole company.

04 Aug – Sep 2025 Racial Equity Here + Colorlines Launch ✓ Complete

Two more sites live: Racial Equity Here and Colorlines launched in parallel sprints. Team coordinated beautifully across delivery streams — proof that the rhythm was locked in.

05 Sep – Oct 2025 Clocking-In Launch — Pixel Perfect ✓ Complete

The fourth site, Clocking-In, launches with such precision that bugs fixed during the migration that had *never* worked on the live D7 site were resolved. Murtaza's "great efficiency, collaboration, and planning" — handling code quality, client feedback, and parallel work simultaneously.

06 Oct 2025 All 4 Sites in CDM ✓ Complete

All four migrated sites now in Continuous Delivery Management. The handoff is official — Axelerant takes the baton for all ongoing operations, monitoring, and improvements.

07 Nov – Dec 2025 EMS Discovery Launch ✓ Complete

New engagement: EMS AI Discovery for Facing Race Conference Event Management System (4–5K attendees, Nov 2026). Prateek's team deployed an AI-driven discovery approach — a thought leadership story waiting to be told.

08 Dec 2025 Overrun CR Approved ✓ Complete

Change Request for scope overrun — approved in the same call where the EMS Discovery engagement was signed. Ulka: "That's the highlight for today for me." Sachin's strategic assist on the call made all the difference in getting both approvals.

09 Jan 2026 Post-Launch QA Stabilisation ✓ Complete

Post-launch stabilisation across all sites. QA Automation work began rolling out to ensure all six properties have the resilience they deserve. Continuous improvement mindset in full effect.

10 Feb 2026 Drupalcon & Acquia Visibility ✓ Complete

Race Forward positioned as a featured reference for Acquia Engage Awards 2026 and selected for Drupalcon's Non-Profit Summit. Thought leadership from a partnership done right.

11 Mar 2026 Goodwill CDM Extension ✓ Complete

Zero-cost CDM extension offered to Race Forward proactively — a trust move, not a friction point. The relationship deepened because we ran as partners, not vendors and clients.

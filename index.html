The Journey

Milestones

Eleven splits. Four launches. Hundreds of decisions. Tap any checkpoint to open the full story.

Split Month Checkpoint Status
01 Mar 2025 SOW Finalized — Race Begins ✓ Complete

SOW finalised for the D7/WP → D11 Managed Migration across four Race Forward sites. Alignment on UAT timelines, invoicing milestones, and support scope locked with Timothy Armstrong. The starting gun had fired.

02 Apr – Jun 2025 Active Delivery + AI Migration Pilot ✓ Complete

Active delivery across 4 sites simultaneously. Team began experimenting with AI-assisted code migration (Codex, Cline + OpenRouter) — an Axelerant first. Estimated 20–25% reduction in backend effort, with gains tracked and shared openly.

03 Aug 2025 First Site Handed to CDM ✓ Complete

Racial Equity Here handed to continuous delivery management — the first of four sites to complete the full migration to Drupal 11. The baton passed cleanly.

04 Aug 28, 2025 Facing Race Goes Live 🎉 🚀 Launch

facingrace.raceforward.org launches. A long-awaited milestone after a journey marked by complexity and grit. Celebrated company-wide in #celebrate. The crowd was watching — the team delivered.

05 Oct 1, 2025 Clocking-In Goes Live 🎉 🚀 Launch

Described internally as "the smoothest development on this account." Nearly pixel-perfect D7 replication — plus bugs fixed that had never worked before. Two other sites were in parallel delivery at the time.

06 Oct 2025 All 4 Sites in Continuous Delivery ✓ Complete

Colorlines and ClockingIn handed to CDM. All four primary Race Forward sites now in continuous delivery management — monitored, maintained, and ready for what comes next.

07 Dec 18, 2025 Double Win in One Call 🏆 🏆 Win

Overrun CR approved and EMS Discovery proposal signed — in the same call with Timothy Armstrong. Ulka's reaction: "That's the highlight for today for me." The baton picked up speed.

08 Dec 2025 Acquia Engage Nomination ✓ Complete

Race Forward submitted as a featured client for Acquia Engage Awards 2026 and the Drupalcon Non-Profit Summit — positioning the partnership on the industry stage.

09 Jan 2026 EMS Discovery Kickoff ● Active

Kickoff of a discovery engagement to build a fully custom Event Management Platform for the Facing Race Conference — 4–5K attendees, November 2026. AI-driven discovery approach, a thought leadership piece in the making.

10 Feb 2026 QA Automation Deal Signed ● Active

QA Automation engagement signed to support quality processes across all 6 Race Forward sites — a step toward a fully resilient digital estate.

11 Mar 2026 New Tech Director + Next Migration Scoped ● In Motion

New Director of Technology onboarded at Race Forward. WordPress → Drupal migration 2026 scoped and under discussion. The race continues — the baton is still moving.
